Shelfy is a mobile app that helps you save and organize screenshots of items or things that catch your interest. It creates a personalized shopping experience by recommending similar products based on your saved screenshots.

Simply take a screenshot of any item you like, and Shelfy will automatically detect it and add it to your personalized shelf. From there, you can browse similar recommendations and shop directly through the app.

Shelfy is for anyone who loves to shop and wants to keep track of the products or trends they see online. Whether you're a casual shopper or an avid deal-hunter, Shelfy helps you stay organized and discover new items with ease.
